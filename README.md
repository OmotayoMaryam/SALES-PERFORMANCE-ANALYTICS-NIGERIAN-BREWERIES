# Project Title
Sales Performance Nigerian Breweries
# Project Overview
Sales performance analysis is critical to understanding profitability and product performance in any Organization. This project uses SQL to analyze profit, country and brand performance for Nigerian Breweries between 2017 and 2019, with a focus on identifying which prodcuts and markets hold a comparative advantage.
# Case Study
Nigerian breweries aim at identifying their best selling product and to achieve this, the marketting Department teamed up with the Data Analytics Department to look into this.The objective is to identify their best selling product and brand, understand sales trends, customer behavior and regional performance across different markets, to improve profitability and market competitiveness. This will be achieved by leveraging a data-driven approach to analysis.For this project, I downloaded my data set from kaggle.
# Tools Used
SQL Server Management Studio
# Data Structure
The data set is a three years sales that consists of 13 columns and 1047 rows.
| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|

| Email | Sales representative's email address |
| Brand | Product brand sold |
| Plant Cost | Cost of production at the plant |
| Unit Price | Selling price per unit |
| Quantity | Number of units sold |
| Cost | Total cost of the transaction |
| Profit | Profit made on the transaction |
| Country | Country where the sale occurred |
| Region | Region within the country |
| Month | Month of the sale |
| Year | Year of the sale |

Prior to Analysis, the data sets were cleaned and validated. This included removing duplicate records,inserting new column,confirming the absence of null values and verifying each column was assigned to appropriate data type.

 The best selling product differ by countries.

 # Reccomendations
 Based on the key findings above, the following recommendations are proposed to help Nigerian Breweries address the observed revenue decline and strengthen its market position across regions and brands.
 1. Investigate the 2019 revenue decline; The steepest drop occured in 2019, conduct a deeper root cause analysis, pricing changes, competitor activity, distribution issues or shift in consumer demand to prevent further decline
 2. Reassess Pricing and cost strategy; since revenue fell while company still posted 241 million Dollars in profit, review whether cost cutting is masking a weakening sales trend, and ensure pricing remains competitive without eroding margins
 3. Tailor product strategy by region; Since Hero performs best in francophone markets and Eagle lager leads in Anglophone markets, allign marketing spend and distribution priorities to each region's preferred brand rather than a one-size fits all approach.
 4. Strenghten Anglophone market investment; Since Anglophone countries consistently generated more profit, consider prioritizing expansion, promotions, or new product launches in these markets while working to close the gap in Francophone countries.
 5. Leverage Hero's brand strength; As the overall best-selling product, consider using Hero as flagship brand in cross-regional campaigns, while still preserving region-specific branding where it performs well(e.g, Eagle lager in Anglophone markets).
 6. Restore growth momentum from 2017 levels; Since 2017 was the peak year, review what strategies, campaigns, or market conditions drove that performance and assess whether they can be replicated or adapted.
# Conclusion
This analysis highlights a consistent decline in Nigerian Breweries' revenue between 2017 and 2019, with the steepest drop occuring in 2019, despite the company maintaining a total profit of 241 million Dollars over the period. Regional and brand level insights reveal clear differences in consumer preference and profitability between Anglophone and Francophone markets, with Hero emerging as the overall best-selling product. These findings provide a data-driven foundation for Nigerian Breweries to refine its pricing, regional strategy, and brand focus going forward.
